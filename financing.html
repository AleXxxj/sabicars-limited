Questions

Frequently Asked

Is the 40% a loan? Do I pay interest?

The 40% Drive Plan is our own in-house arrangement — not a bank loan. You pay 40% upfront and settle the balance on terms we agree together. Separately, we list selected vehicles on AutoCheck, where you can apply for formal financing with their terms. Look for the "AutoCheck Ready" badge on the cards above.

How long do I have to pay the balance?

It depends on the vehicle and your circumstances — we don't force everyone into one schedule. Some clients settle within weeks, others spread it over months. Talk to us on WhatsApp and we'll work out something realistic for you.

Can I really drive the car home the same day?

Yes. Once your 40% clears and documentation is complete, the vehicle is yours to drive. One client paid for a Hiace on a Friday and drove it to Port Harcourt that same day. If you're outside Lagos, we arrange nationwide delivery instead.

What documents do I need?

For the standard plan: valid ID, proof of address, and your payment. For AutoCheck financing, their requirements apply and are listed on their application page. For corporate and fleet orders, we'll guide you through the company documentation needed.

Does this apply to fleet or bulk orders?

Yes — and terms are often more flexible at volume. We've supplied 13 Highlanders and 3 Lexus GX to single clients. If you're buying multiple units, contact us directly and we'll structure something around your budget and timeline.

What happens if I can't complete the balance?

Talk to us early — that's the honest answer. We're a registered Nigerian company (RC 1560100) and we'd rather restructure with you than have a problem. What we can't do is guess; the sooner you tell us, the more options exist.
