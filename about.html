Meet the Founder
Ccristian Dee
Founder & CEO, Sabicars Limited
"Every vehicle that leaves our plaza carries my name on it — accident-free, verified, and exactly as described. That's not a slogan, it's how I built this."

Ccristian Dee — also known to many customers as Ccrist D — founded Sabicars Limited on a simple principle: Nigerian car buyers deserve dealers who tell the truth about what they're selling. What started as a small operation moving individual vehicles has grown into a CAC-registered company (RC 1560100) trusted by both individual buyers and corporate fleet clients across Lagos and beyond.

Under Cchristian Dee's leadership, Sabicars has supplied fleet orders as large as 13 Toyota Highlanders and 3 Lexus GX units to a single corporate client, alongside hundreds of individual sales — all backed by the same verification standard on every unit, regardless of order size.

Today, Sabicars Limited operates from Amazing Grace Plaza on Lasu Road, Igando, with a live online inventory, the signature 40% Drive Plan, and a growing digital presence built to make vehicle buying in Nigeria more transparent than it's ever been.
